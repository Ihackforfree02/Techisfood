About Dragon Fruit

Dragon fruit, also known as pitaya, is a tropical fruit cultivated from several species of cactus. Known for its vibrant pink skin and speckled flesh, it offers a unique blend of flavor and nutrition that appeals to health-conscious consumers worldwide.

Health Benefits

  • Rich in antioxidants and vitamin C
  • Supports digestive health with natural fiber
  • Hydrating and low in calories
  • Contains magnesium and essential nutrients

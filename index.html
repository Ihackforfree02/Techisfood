What's This Crazy Fruit?

Dragon fruit (aka pitaya) is like a fruit from another planet! It grows on cactus vines in hot places. Pink or red skin, green spikes, and inside is white or pink with black seeds that crunch.

  • Home: Mexico & Central America
  • Looks: Like a dragon egg with spikes
  • Tastes like: Kiwi + pear but more fun

From Tiny Seed to Dragon Egg

  1. Baby Plant: Starts as a seed or cutting (like a plant clone!)
  2. Growing Up: Climbs up poles like a jungle vine (so cool!)
  3. Flower Power: Huge white flowers that only open at night (like magic!)
  4. Fruit Time: Tiny fruit grows for about a month into dragon eggs
  5. Harvest: Farmers pick them when they're perfectly pink and ready

Super Easy Dragon Fruit Recipes

Dragon Power Smoothie Bowl

Makes: 2 bowls

Stuff You Need:

  • 1 cup frozen dragon fruit
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup coconut milk
  • Toppings: granola, chia seeds, fruit

How to Make It:

  1. Blend everything until smooth
  2. Pour into bowls and add toppings
  3. Eat with a spoon - yum!

Dragon Fruit Popsicles

Makes: 6 pops

Stuff You Need:

  • 2 cups dragon fruit mush
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • ½ cup coconut water
  • Popsicle sticks

How to Make It:

  1. Mash the dragon fruit
  2. Mix with honey & coconut water
  3. Pour into molds and freeze 4 hours
  4. Enjoy your dragon pops!

What is Dragon Fruit?

Dragon fruit, also known as pitaya, is a tropical fruit known for its vivid pink skin and speckled flesh. It's rich in antioxidants, vitamin C, and fiber.

Health Benefits

  • Boosts immune system
  • Supports digestion
  • Promotes healthy skin
  • Low in calories, high in nutrients

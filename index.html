What's This Crazy Fruit?

Dragon fruit (pitaya) grows on cactus vines in hot places. Pink skin with green spikes, inside it's white or pink with tiny black seeds that crunch!

  • Home: Mexico & Central America
  • Looks: Like a dragon egg
  • Taste: Kiwi + pear combo

About Dragon Fruit

Dragon fruit, or pitaya, is a tropical cactus fruit known for its vibrant pink skin and speckled flesh. It offers a unique blend of flavor and nutrition, making it a favorite among health-conscious consumers and culinary creatives alike.

Health Benefits

  • Rich in antioxidants and vitamin C
  • Supports digestive health with natural fiber
  • Hydrating and low in calories
  • Contains magnesium and essential nutrients

About Dragon Fruit

Dragon fruit, also known as pitaya, is a cactus fruit prized for its striking color, subtle sweetness, and versatility in both sweet and savoury dishes. We partner with growers who follow regenerative practices to ensure flavour and soil health.

  • Bake into desserts or make a refreshing sorbet

About Dragon Fruit

The Tropical Wonder

Dragon fruit, scientifically known as Hylocereus undatus, is a tropical fruit that grows on a climbing cactus. Native to Central America but now cultivated worldwide, this exotic fruit is loved for its unique appearance and refreshing taste.

The fruit comes in three main varieties: white-fleshed with pink skin, red-fleshed with pink skin, and white-fleshed with yellow skin. Each variety has its own distinct flavor profile and nutritional benefits.

Fun Facts

Night Bloomer

Dragon fruit flowers bloom only at night and typically last just one evening.

Bat Pollinated

In the wild, dragon fruit flowers are primarily pollinated by bats and moths.

Long-Lived

The dragon fruit cactus can live and produce fruit for up to 20 years.

Hydrating

Dragon fruit is about 90% water, making it incredibly hydrating.

Global Journey

Native to Central America, it's now widely grown in Southeast Asia.

Heart Healthy

The tiny black seeds contain omega-3 and omega-6 fatty acids.
